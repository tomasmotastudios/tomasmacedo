About Tomás Macedo

Tomás Macedo is a professional football player currently playing for União de Leiria. He has shown exceptional talent and commitment both on and off the field.

Born in Portugal, Macedo started his football journey at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks due to his skill, vision, and dedication.

Carrer Highlights

  • Started professional career with União de Leiria
  • Top scorer in youth championships
  • Key player in multiple regional tournaments
